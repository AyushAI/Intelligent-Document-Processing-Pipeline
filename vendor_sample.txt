VENDOR SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Vendor Supply Agreement is made between Orion Retail Pvt Ltd ("Buyer") and Global Packaging Industries ("Vendor").

The Vendor agrees to supply eco-friendly packaging materials for a period of 12 months beginning February 2026.

The Buyer agrees to purchase a minimum of 10,000 units per quarter.

Delivery Terms:
All goods must be delivered within 14 days of purchase order issuance.

Quality Assurance:
All materials must comply with ISO 9001 standards.

Pricing:
Unit price is fixed at $2.50 per unit for the first 6 months, subject to renegotiation thereafter.

Dispute Resolution:
Any disputes shall first be resolved through mediation before arbitration proceedings.

Both parties agree to cooperate in good faith to ensure smooth execution of this agreement.
