ANNUAL COMPLIANCE REPORT – 2025

Company: Orion Retail Pvt Ltd
Reporting Period: January 2025 – December 2025

1. Data Protection Compliance
The company implemented GDPR-aligned policies; however, audit findings revealed incomplete encryption practices in legacy systems.

2. Financial Reporting
Quarterly reports were submitted; however, delays of up to 15 days were observed in Q2 and Q3.

3. Vendor Screening
Vendor due diligence was conducted for 75% of active vendors. The remaining 25% lack updated compliance documentation.

4. Cybersecurity
Two phishing incidents occurred in March 2025. Incident response procedures were followed but not formally documented.

5. Regulatory Filings
One late filing was identified with the state regulatory authority.

Conclusion:
While the company demonstrates general compliance efforts, several operational and regulatory risks remain.
